# Ken's Job Tracker 2026

A simple, clean web app for tracking job applications. No account needed, no internet required — it runs right in your browser.

---

## How to use it

1. **Open the file** — double-click `index.html` to open it in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge all work great)
2. **Add a job** — click the green **+ Add Job** button in the top right
3. **Fill in the details:**
   - Company name (required)
   - Job title (required)
   - Date you applied
   - Status (see below)
   - Notes — anything helpful like a contact name or next steps
4. **Save it** — click the Save button
5. **Update anytime** — click the ✏️ pencil icon to edit, or 🗑️ to remove

---

## Status options

| Color | Status | What it means |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| 🔵 Blue | Applied | You submitted the application |
| 🟣 Purple | Waiting to hear back | Application is in, no reply yet |
| 🟠 Orange | Interview scheduled | They want to meet you! |
| 🟢 Green | Got an offer! | Congratulations! |
| 🔴 Red | Not selected | They went another direction |

---

## Filtering

Use the filter buttons below the summary to see only certain types — for example, click **Interview** to see all your upcoming interviews at a glance.

---

## Your data stays on your computer

Everything is saved in your browser's local storage — nothing is sent to the internet. Your applications will still be there the next time you open the file, as long as you use the same browser on the same computer.

> **Tip:** Don't clear your browser history/data or it may erase your saved jobs. If you want to back up your data, you can copy the jobs list to a Notes app or Google Doc periodically.

---

## Requirements

- Any modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari)
- No internet connection needed after downloading
- Works on Windows, Mac, and most phones/tablets
